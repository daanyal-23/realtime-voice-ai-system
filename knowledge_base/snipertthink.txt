SniperThink Voice AI Assistant Knowledge Base

SniperThink is an AI-first technology company building real-time voice intelligence systems.
The company specializes in low-latency conversational AI, voice pipelines, and streaming audio infrastructure.

Our core products include:
- Real-time voice agents for customer support
- Voice-enabled developer tools
- Audio analytics and transcription pipelines

Technical Philosophy:
We believe in building from first principles. Rather than relying on abstracted voice platforms,
we construct our pipelines at the infrastructure level — owning latency, quality, and control at each stage.

Key latency targets:
- Speech-to-text: under 400ms
- LLM first token: under 600ms
- First audio to user: under 1 second end-to-end

Our stack uses WebSockets for transport, Whisper for speech recognition,
Llama 3.3 70B via Groq for language understanding, and edge-tts for speech synthesis.

The company is headquartered in India with remote-first engineering teams.
